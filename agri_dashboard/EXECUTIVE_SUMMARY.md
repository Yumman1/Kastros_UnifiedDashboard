# Executive Summary - Pakistan Commodities Trading Dashboard

## What Is This Application?

A **comprehensive AI-powered trading intelligence platform** for Pakistan's agricultural commodities market (Cotton, Wheat, Corn). It helps traders make better decisions through real-time data, predictive analytics, and automated opportunity detection.

---

## Key Features (5 Main Tabs)

### 📈 **Tab 1: Live Rates**
- Today's market prices across all cities
- Real-time price monitoring
- Filter by commodity and city

### 📊 **Tab 2: Trends**
- Historical price charts
- Identify patterns and seasonal trends
- Compare commodity performance

### 🤖 **Tab 3: AI Forecast**
- **7-day price predictions** using machine learning
- Confidence intervals (shows prediction reliability)
- Helps anticipate market movements

### 💰 **Tab 4: Arbitrage Scanner**
- **Automatically finds profitable trading opportunities**
- Compares prices between cities
- Highlights opportunities with >5% profit margin
- Shows best trading routes

### 📰 **Tab 5: Market Intelligence**
- News sentiment analysis
- Bullish/Bearish signals
- Market psychology insights

## Technology

- **Platform:** Desktop application (runs on your computer)
- **Database:** Local SQLite (no cloud, all data stays private)
- **AI:** Facebook Prophet for forecasting
- **Interface:** Web-based dashboard (easy to use)
